When it comes to summer salads, few dishes are as refreshing and satisfying as a creamy cucumber salad. This simple yet vibrant dish is perfect for picnics, barbecues, or as a light side dish for any meal.

With just a few ingredients and minimal prep time, you can whip up this delicious salad in no time. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the recipe for creamy cucumber salad and explore the flavors and textures that make it so irresistible.

The Process

  1. To make the dressing, combine the sour cream, lemon juice, sugar, dill, and salt in a bowl. Set the dressing aside.
  2. Peel the cucumbers, if desired. Slice the cucumbers and thinly slice the red onions.
  3. Combine the cucumber, red onion, and dressing in a large bowl and stir to combine.
  4. Let the salad sit for 5-10 minutes, give it another stir, then serve.
